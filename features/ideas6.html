Valentine’s Day Fundraiser – Kissing and Hissing Booth

Feb 10, 2023

Sample Social Media Post:

Mark your calendars for this and come to to support the homeless animals of .

There will be a fun night of puppy kisses for you Valentine’s Day lovers! Not a fan of Valentine’s Day? Donate to the hissing booth instead!

We’re asking for $1 donation for one kiss or one hiss!

    Tips
  • Bring extra treats for the dog in the booth, they’ll get bored
  • Instead of having real cats, have a sound machine with hissing noises
      • Add On’s
  • Have Valentine’s day cards for people to “sponsor” shelter pets with a donation
  • Host at a bar or restaurant and ask for a percent of proceeds raised in food and beverage that evening donated to the shelter
  • Cat litter tray – write your ex’s name at the bottom of the litter box and we’ll send you a picture of our cat’s using it!


      • Real Shelter Results
  • Yancey Humane Society raised $200 in hissing and kissing booth sales in a 4 hour period, raised an additional $200 from the restaurant that donated a portion of sales from food and bev for the day.
