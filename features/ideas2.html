Silent Auction

Feb 21, 2023



Grab your best spy gadgets, brush up on your secret handshake skills, and join us for an evening of music, good food, and dancing at Savannah Station! This annual auction hosted by One Love Animal Rescue and Savannah Station replaces the well-recognized Friends of Animals Annual Auction.

Report On: Saturday, August 13, 2022, from 6:00 pm – 10:00 pm.

Report To: Savannah Station, 601 Cohen St., Savannah, GA 31401

What to Wear: Your best spy gear (or whatever you’re comfortable in)

Tickets: $45 per person. Tickets can be purchased by visiting charityauction.bid/MissionPawsible

Your admission ticket includes a spy-tacular evening of entertainment and dancing, two drink tickets, and culinary tastings of Savannah.

Silent Auction – Participate during the event or online. Online bidding starts August 3! Visit charityauction.bid/MissionPawsible to register.

No ticket needed to participate online.

50/50 Raffle

Photo Booth

Best Dressed Competition

COVID-19 Precautions

Can’t be there in person? You can still accept this mission by bidding online! Bid from the comfort of your own home (or wherever you may be) starting August 3rd through the close of the auction at 9:00pm on August 13th. No ticket purchase is necessary to participate online.
