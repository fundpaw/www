YHS Dog Wash Event

Sample:

Come out and join us the week of June 19 th from 4:30pm-6:30pm for our dog wash fundraiser! Dogs get dirty, and sometimes your precious pup will need a professional bath to get clean. But with a busy schedule and an excitable pet, finding the time and space to wash your dog can be a challenge. 

While we wash you pup, sit back and relax at our human snack station OR shop our barkery for some treats for your pets!

For each dog we wash, we ask for a donation of $15 Tips

Make sure all dogs are on a leash the WHOLE time so they don’t escape Add On’s

Have an area for animal parent’s to hangout in that’s shaded so they can relax while they wait

Have snacks and drinks for sale for animal parents to purchase or complimentary with their dog wash

Have a barkery and make/buy treats to sell for animal parents to buy for their pets

Ask a local food truck to set up shop outside of your event to draw more people in
